About me

I am an industrial design student passionate about blending robust engineering with eye-pleasing aesthetics. My background includes several years of building competition robots and developing tangible products that feature electronics.

At Eindhoven University of Technology I refined my skills across the entire design process—from analysis and ideation to prototyping with great attention to detail.

Vision

I believe in designing meaningful products that strike a balance between the digital and physical worlds. Technology should enrich everyday life without demanding all of our attention.
