UNIVERSITY PROJECTS

ARCEE, THE ROAMING ROBOT

Arcee is a robotic vehicle designed to avoid obstacles. Its purpose is to drive peacefully on its own without hitting anything. When approaching an obstacle, 
Arcee detects it and manoeuvres to avoid it.

TEMPESTIO CHANDALERT

Tempestio is a smart chandelier that combines rumbling sounds, striking light effects as well as flowy fabric motion to bring the experience of the upcoming weather to the home, before it comes.
